TO THE LOVE OF MY LIFE

(HAPPY BIRTHDAY)

Dear Love,

you steal the blanket, eat my food,

and still make my life sweeter every day.

You make me laugh when I don’t want to,

calm me when I’m about to lose it, and love

me in a way that feels warm and safe.

How is that even possible?

Happy birthday, my Pookie

I love you with all my heart
